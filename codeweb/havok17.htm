Here is a little list of stuff fixxed and implemented with havoks 1.7 release a few months back.

HAVOKMUD 1.7

Be Aware that Rare items ARE being tallied at the Inn. 23 rares
at the most at level 50. All levels of players will get more rares
than previously.

By Popular Demand
=================
-conjure elemental will now summon the correct elemental
-Detect Good will show the white aura surrounding good people
-protection from good and prot from good group are now available
-Identify has been fixxed so that Race and Align slay weapons tell you
what they do. Also immortals will get a little extra info
-memorize modified for sorcs. (att also tells more about the mem)
-brittle keys scrap after use.
-Knock can now be made into staves and wands
-put all bag now works

Cool Stuff
==========
-Wizlist is now properly centered
-kick is now dark yellow
-wdisplay has been fixxed
-You can now see how full your bags are
-Elves have a chance to see secret doors
-Closed doors are now more apparent
-Changed inventory manipulation messages slightly here and there.
-"Terminate with @" Should be a thing of the past, the ending character
for the editor should be posted as ~
-Shout now costs only 5 move and mana
-the OOC (Out of Character channel) is now reserved only for immortals.
-Forest Gnomes now wander the realms. They are the only race allowed to
be Druid/Rangers.
-The land and launch commands now give you greater control over your flight.
-Avariel can now be seen flying overhead. This includes changes to second
wind and refresh as they pertain to wings

Stuff you didn't even know was broke
====================================
-Cloaks will now properly add AC apply to your armor
-protection from evil now properly gives you a +1 to hit evil creatures,
and them a -4 to hit you. You are also protected from a ghoul's para attack.
