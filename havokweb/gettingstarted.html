A Newbie's Guide to Havok

Welcome!

These pages have been designed with the newbie in mind.  If you're new to Havok - and particularly if you're new to MUDing - you will find the links below to be invaluable towards getting your adventuring carreer off to a good start.  They will talk you through the first crucial steps of learning.  However!  Don't be afraid to ask for help in-game, either from the other players, or from the Immortal Staff members - we're here to help!  Let us know if there's anything we can do to help make your experience with us more comfortable.  You're our guest.

Also, keep in mind, you can get automated help in-game with the help command.  We're currently updating our help system, so you might stumble across subjects that aren't listed - thankfully, our game compiles a list of missing subjects as people find them, so we know what to add in!
