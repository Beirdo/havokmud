Info for starting your own clan:

Starting your own clan:
To start your own clan, you must be a Legend (which required 200 million leadership experience), have three people willing to follow you, and have exhibited consistent behavior not only in abiding by the rules of Havok, but also showing an understanding of the spirit in which the rules were created.  The cost of founding a clan is 25 million gold coins.

What you get:
   - A clan hall consisting of an entry hall, connected to a city that is easily walked to, or astralled to.
   - A locked entrace with keys provided to members.
   - An inn, including an innkeeper of the race and alignment of the clan's choice.
   - Induction as a clan (ie. listed in clan list, eligible to participate in clan activities such as clan vs. clan arenas, clan vs. clan quests, and other clan-only activities.

Optional Additions:
   - A bank, including a banker.  This is a normal bank, but will be connected to your clan hall.  Cost is 5 million gold coins.
   - A meeting room.  This room can be decorated and named any way you like.  The meeting room will contain a private message board for the clan's use.  Cost is 10 million gold coins.
   - A general store.  The general store will include a shopkeeper that sells items such as bread, waterskins, backpacks, lanterns, torches, and any other items of this sort that you get approved.  Cost is 5 million gold coins.
   - Alchemy shop.  This store is a shop, with a shopkeeper, that sells up to 6 potions from the following list: shield, armor, strength, aid, bless, detect invis, true sight, detect magic, detect good, detect evil, cure poison, cure light, cure serious, cure critic, heal, second wind, sense life, mana.  If you want a potion in your shop that is not on this list, ask about it and we may or may not approve it.  The cost of this shop is 15 million gold coins.
   - A portal room which allows up to four portals that lead to approved innkeepers of the clan's choice.  The cost is 20 million gold coins.
   - An arena that functions just like the regular arena, but is always open to your clan.  Cost is 5 million gold coins per room.
   - A guard that will allow only clan members to enter the clan hall.  This eliminates the need for a locked door and keys.  The cost is 10 million gold coins.
   - The portal package includes a portal item that is carried in the inventory of every clan member.  You can enter this portal to return to your clanhall from anywhere in the world.  The portal will be replaced free of charge to clanmembers who lose theirs.  The cost is 50 million gold coins.
   - Regen Room which is a flag set on an existing room that will cause increased regeneration rates for the players in the room.  This does NOT include a new room, but is added to a room you allready have.  The cost is 5 million gold coins per room.
   - Extra Rooms may be added at the cost of 3 million gold coins per room.  These rooms will not have any "special" flags such as save, regen, teleport, etc.
