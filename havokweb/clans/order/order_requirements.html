Requirements to join The Order

Currently The Order is accepting new members.  In order to join the applicant must meet the following requirements:

  1. Be sponsored by a current member of The Order.

  2. Undergo a brief interview period where he or she must lead group with each of the current clan members (not required to group with us all at once).

  3. Show intimate knowledge of one hero level zone (approved by clan leadership) by leading one or more members through it successfully and explaining the ins and outs of the zone (secrets, item stats, mob progression, tricks, etc.).

  4. Be accepted by a simple majority vote of current members.

  5. Finally, if all other requirements are met, the applicant must provide 10 million gold to the clan coffers.  Half of which will go towards creating a personalized room for your character.  The other half will go to the party discretionary fund.

While these requirements may change, the current requirements as listed here when you apply are the application requirements for your character.  That is...if these change and you are still under review you don't have to do the new stuff just what was listed here when you first applied.
