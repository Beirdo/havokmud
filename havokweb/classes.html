Character Classes

HavokMUD has a various number of playable Classes.  Most of these classes can also be paired together to play dual- and tri-classes but are obviously much harder to play.  New Players to the mud should stick to single classes and work their way up.

Note that the minimum stats listed are NOT requirements - they are recommendations we make that will make your character better able to deal with what s/he needs.

Mages
Minimum Intelligence: 9
Mages are the class that uses powerful and violent magic, but the magic user does not control nature, and can therefore not bless and such. 

Clerics
Minimum Wisdom: 9
Clerics have the wisdom and blessings of the Gods required to perform Blessings and Healings, although many of them also learn more violent spells.

Warriors
Minimum Strength: 9
Warriors are known to have more strength than the average player.  This makes them better at hand-to-hand combat, as they possess skills of violence and fighting unequaled by the members of any other class.  However, they lack the ability to use magic of any sort.

Thieves
Minimum Dexterity: 9
Thieves are known to have very special qualities, that no other class offers.  Many of these qualities can come in handy in many situations, such as picking locks.  They are not very combat oriented, however, so do not expect to be able to kill many things by yourself.  The useful aspect of thieves involves their ability to scout ahead, avoid monsters, detect and remove traps, and work in the black market.

Paladins
Minimum Strength: 12, Minimum Constitution: 9
Minimum Wisdom: 13, Minimum Charisma: 17
Holy warrios, protectors of the church and the general lawful goodies that most evil creatures HATE.  Paladins must remain in good standings (align must be 350+).

Paladins have an innate ability to detect evil and have protection from evil.  They also have some special skills that they and only they can use.

Rangers
Minimum Strength: 13, Minimum Dexterity: 14
Minimum Wisdom: 14
Rangers are warriors of the woods. They are expert trackers and learn the ways to best protect and live in a woodland environment. A Ranger's alignment must never become evil (below -350).

Rangers have semi-Cleric/Druid abilities. As the Ranger progresses s/he can learn specific spells that have to do with forest/animal training. To use the spells you use the cast command. 

Most skills that a Ranger uses must be used OUTSIDE. OUTSIDE is considered in a forest, desert, artic or anywhere not inside.

Druids
Minimum Wisdom: 12, Minimum Charisma: 15
Druids are spell casters who are in tune with the balance of nature.  They have several Cleric-like spells, a few Mage-like spells, and many spells unique to the class.  A Druid's alignment must always be neutral (between 350 and -350)

Barbarians
Minimum Strength: 15, Minimum Dexterity: 14
Minimum Constitution: 15
Barbarians are big brutes who cannot use ANY magical items.  They are very similar to the Warrior class, but they have higher hit-point bonuses.  One of the defining characteristics of Barbarians is that they are frenzied fighters.

Psionists
Minimum Constitution: 11, Minimum Intelligence: 12
Minimum Wisdom: 15
Psionist are characters that use the power of the mind. They can summon people from great distances, they have even been known to blast the mind of powerful beings to jelly!

Psionist use the command MIND to activate most of their skills, be sure to see the help section on MIND.

Monks
Minimum Strength: 15, Minimum Dexterity: 15
Minimum Constitution: 11, Minimum Wisdom: 15
Monks are a sub-class of fighters which are very quick and agile. They use various attack forms taught by Cain the monk guildmaster at the monk monastry. Monks concentrate on becoming harmonious not only with themselves, but with nature, giving them some advantages in fighting and, over time,  skills which reflect this harmony. Furthermore, at very high levels, Monks develop natural resistances to certain sources of damage. Monks don't generally use weapons or shields, for they hit harder with there bare hands.

At high levels Monks have been known to get up to 5 regular attacks,

Bards
Minimum Dexterity: 12, Minimum Intelligence: 13
Minimum Charisma: 15
A rogue who makes his way in life by his charm, talent, and wit.  They are the Jack of All Trades, yet Master of none.

Bards have been temporarily discontinued while we sort out their place in the world that will best promote balance.
