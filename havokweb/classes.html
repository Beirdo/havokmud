Class Information


HavokMUD has a various number of playable Classes.  Most of these classes can also be paired together to play dual and Tri-classes but are obviously much harder to play.  New Players to the mud should stick to single classes and work there way up.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Mage
Desired Stats: -- Str,  -- Dex, -- Con, 9 Int, -- Wis, -- Chr
Special Notes: None
Description: This is the class that uses powerful and violent magic, but the magic user does not control the nature, and can therefore not bless and such. 

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Cleric
Desired Stats: -- Str, -- Dex, -- Con, -- Int, 9 Wis, -- Chr
Special Notes: None
Description: This class gives you the wisdom of peace, blessing and healing, though it is possible to learn more violent spells. 

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Warrior
Desired Stats: 9 Str, -- Dex, -- Con, -- Int, -- Wis, -- Chr
Special Notes: None
Description: Warriors are known to have more strength than the average player.  This makes them better at hand-to-hand-combat, as they possess skills of violence and fighting unequaled by the members of any other class. However, they lack the ability to use magic of any sort.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Thief
Desired Stats: -- Str, 9 Dex, -- Con, -- Int, -- Wis, -- Chr
Special Notes: None
Description: Thieves are known to have very special qualities, that no other class offers. Many of these qualities can come in handy in many situations, such as picking locks. They are not very combat oriented, however, so do not expect to be able to kill many things by yourself.  The useful aspect of thieves involves their ability to scout ahead, avoiding monsters, detecting and removing traps and selling and buying items at the black market.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Paladin
Desired Stats: 12 Str, -- Dex, 9 Con, -- Int, 13 Wis, 17 Chr
Special Notes: Good Aligned only
Description: Holy warriors, protectors of the church and the general lawful goodies that most evil creatures HATE. Paladins must remain in good standings (align must be 350+). 

Paladins have an innate ability of detecting evil and protection from evil. They also have some special skills that they and only they can use.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Ranger
Desired Stats: 13 Str, 13 Dex, 14 Con, -- Int, 14 Wis, -- Chr
Special Notes: Good and Neutral Aligned Only
Description: Rangers are warriors of the woods. They are expert trackers and learn the ways to best protect and live in a woodland environment. Rangers alignment must never become evil (below -350).

Rangers have semi-cleric/druid abilities. As the ranger progresses s/he can learn specific spells that have to do with forest/animal training. To use the spells you use the cast command. 

Most skills that a ranger uses must be used OUTSIDE. OUTSIDE is considered in a forest, desert, artic or anywhere no inside.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Druid
Desired Stats: -- Str, -- Dex, -- Con, -- Int, 12 Wis, 15 Chr
Special Notes: Must Remain Neutral in Alignment
Description: Spell casters in tune with the balance of nature.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Barbarian
Desired Stats: 15 Str, 14 Dex, 15 Con, -- Int, -- Wis, -- Chr
Special Notes: Good / Evil / Neutral
Description: Big fighters who cannot use any magic items.  Very similar to the warrior class but Barbarians have higher Hit point bonuses.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Psionist
Desired Stats: -- Str, -- Dex, 11 Con, 12 Int, 15 Wis, -- Chr
Special Notes: Uses Mind instead of Cast
Description: Psionist are characters that use the power of the mind. They can summon people from great distances, they have even been known to blast the mind of powerful beings to jelly!

Psionist use the command MIND to activate most of their skills, be sure to see the help section on MIND.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Monk
Desired Stats: 15 Str, 15 Dex, 11 Con, -- Int, 15 Wis, -- Chr
Special Notes: None
Description: Monks are a sub-class of fighters which are very quick and agile. They use various attack forms taught by Cain the monk guildmaster at the monk monastry. Monks concentrate on becoming harmonious not only with themselves, but with nature, giving them some advantages in fighting and, over time,  skills which reflect this harmony. Furthermore, at very high levels, monks develop natural resistances to certain sources of damage. Monks don't generally use weapons or shields, for they hit harder with there bare hands.

At high levels monks have been known to get up to 5 regular attacks,

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Class: Bard
Desired Stats: -- Str, 12 Dex, -- Con, 13 Int, -- Wis, 15 Chr
Special Notes: Not Playable yet.
Description: A rogue who makes his way in life by his charm, talent, and wit.  They are the Jack of all trade yet master of none.
