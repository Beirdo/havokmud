Welcome Everyone to Havok's Official Website.  Havok is a Text Based Multi-User-Dimension where people from all over the world join together to meet new people and have lots of Fun.

If you are new to mudding and have no clue as to what is need in order to get started then please read the Getting Started guide, or if your ready to play then please click the Play Havok button to the left.
