Newbie Guide: Connecting to Havok
 

Havok, as do all MUDs, relies on an internet protocol named "Telnet".  Telnet is one of the oldest networking technologies.  It is very simple.  Your computer most likely already has a telnet application installed on it, but most of these are very simple, and many of them don't support color, which can make the game much more enjoyable.  We have several options listed below for what are called "MUD Clients", which basically are suped-up telnet applications.

MUD Clients for Windows:

zMUD
The most popular MUD client for Windows is called zMUD.  It has been in development for years and years, and includes it's own scripting language.  The newer versions have a 30-day trial, but the older versions (which will do everything you need) are now freeware.
