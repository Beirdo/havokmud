Newbie Guide: Grouping
 

Grouping is nice, firstly because it makes it easier to beat things up if you have them outnumbered, but also because it's fun to have someone to play with.

To group with someone, first, decide who will be the leader.  Everyone else has to type follow (name of the leader).  The group leader then types group all (or you can do group (playername) to get a specific person) and everyone will be in the group.

Once you're in a group, you can type group to see who's in it and how beat up they are.

You can also use group talk, which is used like: gt Wow, you guys are the greatest groupmates ever!

If you type gwho, you'll see all the other existing groups in the game, and who's in them.  The group leader can type set group name (new name of the group) to change your group's name.
