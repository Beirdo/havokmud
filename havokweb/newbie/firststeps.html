Newbie Guide: First Steps
 

Your character's adventure starts in the Temple of Karsinya, which is the main city in the world.  To move around, type directions: North, South, East, West, Up, or Down.  These can be abbreviated as N, S, E, W, U, D.

Before you begin, you should set yourself a new command prompt.  Type help prompt to see all the different options, but for now you can just type prompt 10 to use my personal favorite preset.

Your first set of equipment
The first thing you'll want to do is get yourself a set of equipment.  You can check what equipment you're wearing by typing equipment, or eq for short.  To see the items you're holding in your inventory, type inventory, or i.You can find your first set of equipment in the Newbie Training Zone, which begins one room west from the Temple of Karsinya.  Read the room descriptions in that zone carefully - they will help you figure out what equipment you need.

Most equipment in Havok - including the equipment in the Newbie Training Zone - is found on NPCs, which are Non-Player Characters (i.e., characters that the game controlls).  To get a  piece of equipment from an NPC, you usually will have to kill him for it.

Combat
To initiate combat with an NPC, type kill (mobname). (The word "mob" in MUDs is used to refer to NPCs.  It's short for "mobile", which describes a creature that can move around, although many mobs are programed to stand still.)

Alternatively, you can initiate combat with a combat skill, such as bash or kick, by typing bash (mobname).  Since you've just started out though, you haven't learned any skills.

Before you attack a mob, you should size it up a bit.  Use the consider command, as in: consider (mobname) to see what your chances are of beating him!

Picking up and using your new equipment
If you type look after you've killed your first mob, you'll see that there is now a corpse in the room.  Type examine corpse or exa corpse to see if he had anything (you can also exa (mobname) while he's still alive to see if he's wearing anything).  In the Newbie Training Zone, every mob has equipment for you to use.  Type get all corpse to get everything off a corpse.  You can also type get (item) corpse to get a specific item off a corpse.

If there are two or more corpses in a room, use get all 2.corpse or get all 3.corpse, depending on which one needs looting.

Also, you can type autoloot, autogold, and autosplit to use some of the built-in macros.  Autoloot will automatically loot everything from every mob you kill.  Autogold will take any money they might have.  Autosplit will automatically split gold you pick up equally between each member of your group, if you're in a group.

Now that you've gotten some equipment, type wear all or wear (item) to equip it.  Weapons get equipped with wield (item).

Go through the rest of the Newbie Training Zone and get yourself a complete set of equipment.  By the time you're done, you will probably have gotten enough experience to gain your first level, which is discussed in the next chapter: Gaining Levels, Learning Skills, and Casting Spells.
