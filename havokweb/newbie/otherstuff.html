Newbie Guide: Other Stuff
 

Exiting the game
To exit the game, you have two options, but only one is desireable.  The first is to type quit now, which will exit you straight out of the game, immediately.  However, if you use this option, all of your equipment and money will fall to the ground, and you'll lose them.

The other option (which is the way you're supposed to do it) is to rent your character a room in one of the local inns.  This will save your character's equipment and give them a safe place to sleep while you're off adventuring in the Real World.

The Karsinya Inn is located just one room east from the Meeting Square, and Rosetta the Receptionist is one room up from there.  You can type offer while in the Reception to see how much money it will cost you to rent, and you can type rent to leave the game.

Hurry back now!

Various Useful Commands
You can type score to see some useful info about your character.
You can also type attributes or att to see some other info.  After level 6, att will show you your character's stats.
