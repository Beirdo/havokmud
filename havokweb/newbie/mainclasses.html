Newbie Guide: Main Classes

Mages
- Main class mages have the brew command, which allows them to
create potions of mage spells.
- Additional spell: mana shield, which applies physical damage taken
by the mage to their mana pool before taking off hit points. The damage
dealt to the mana pool ignores factors such as resist, suscept, and sanctuary.
- Bonus +10 mana regeneration.

Clerics
- Main class clerics have the scribe command, which allows them to
create scrolls of cleric spells.
- Additional spell: group heal.
- Bonus duration length of +1 tick on blade barrier and sanctuary durations.

Warriors
- Extra weapon proficiencies.
- Skill bellow becomes an area affect bash.
- Bonus +8 hitpoint regeneration.
- Bonus +5 move regeneration.
Additional skill: mend, which can be used to repair damaged armor and weaponry.

Thieves
- Main class thieves have a higher backstab damage bonus that other thieves.  This bonus is the same as all thieves had before the Havok 2 release - non-main classes suffered a damage reduction.
- When main class thieves retreat/flee, they get two automatic hide 7 attempts.

Druids
- Additional spell: iron skins, duration: level*1.5. This spell protects the
caster from damage - it absorbs damage (ignoring resist/suscupts/sanc), and it slowly crumbles as it does until it's destroyed.
- Additional spell: creeping death.
- Bonus +5 mana regeneration.

Monks
- Receive dodge bonuses from fighting styles.
- Max attacks go to five per round at level 50.
- Additional skill: flurry of blows, which has 3-4 rounds of lag, and increases the monk's number of attacks by 30% for one round.
- Additional skill: flower fist, which is an area attack.

Barbarians
- Immunity to poison at level 20.
- 1% harder skin per level - at level 50, barbs get a pierce damage reduction of 75%, added to any damage modifiers.
- Bonus +8 move regeneration.
- Bonus +5 hitpoint regeneration.

Rangers
- When hasted, dual-wielded weapons get an extra attack.
- Bonus +10% damage vs. undead.
- Bonus +5% damage vs. evil.

Paladins
- Bonus +10% damage vs. undead.
- Bonus +20% damage vs. evil.
- Penalty -20% damage vs. good.
- Bonus 15% discount on rent.

Psionists
- Immune to sleep spells.
- Immune to charm spells.
- Bonus +5 hitpoint regeneration.
- Bonus +5 mana regeneration.
- Ability to use the scry command on objects.
- Bonus telekinetic knock spell, to unlock doors.

Necromancers
- Penalty -10% damage vs. undead.
- Bonus +15% damage vs. good.
- Bonus +5% damage vs. neutral.
- Penalty -20% damage vs. evil.
