Newbie Guide: What is a MUD?
 

The original meaning of the achronym, "MUD" has probably been lost to the world.  Depending on who you ask, people will tell you that it means "Multi-User Dungeon", "Multi-User Domain", "Multi-User Dimension", or any number of others.  The bottom line is that a MUD is a text world where people from all over the world connect via the internet protocol "Telnet" and have fun with each other.

While many MUDs are very different from one another, most of them have one common element: You can group up with other adventurers and wander the realms of fantasy slaying monsters, capturing damsel princesses, roleplaying, or really anything you want to do.
