Appendix I: Skills and Spells (ALLSPELLS)

  For a complete walk-through, check out the Guide to Building on Havok: these pages are intended as quick-references.

 This next section is stripped straight out of allspells, which lists every skill and spell in the game, the levels of each class that can use it, and most importantly, the SPELL NUMBER, which is what you use to tell the mud what spell or skill you’re talking about.  For example, if you want an item to have a spell affect: giant growth, you’d refer to spell# 205.  Keep in mind that every skill and spell is in this list, and not all of them are applicable to everything.  For instance, you shouldn’t have a spell affect: kick.  It just doesn’t make any sense.

Use CTRL-F to quickly find a spell.  (I don't know what it is on Mac, prolly CMND-F or something.)
