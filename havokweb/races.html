Player Races

Havok has grown to become a very vast world.  Many different creatures and exotic races populate much of this world, all having their own traits and abilities .  As many as 132 races have been known to trek the lands.  To see a list of every race, type help allrace while you're on Havok.  But for a list and description of the most common playable races, see below.

For a big chart that lists the level limits of every race, click here.

Class Abbreviations:
ma=magic user, cl=cleric, wa=warrior, th=thief, dr=druid, mk=monk
ba=barbarian, so=sorcerer, pa=paladin, ra=ranger, ps=psionist, ne=necromancer

Dwarves
Playable Classes: cl (level 50), wa (level 50), th (level 35), pa (level 45), ra (level 30)
Available Multiclasses: wa/th, cl/wa
Max Stats: 17 dex, 18 int, 18 wis, 19 con, 17 chr, 18 str
Dwarves are known for their skill in warfare, their ability to withstand physical and magical punishment, their knowledge of the earth's secrets, their hard work, and their capacity for drinking ale. They stand only 4 to 4 1/2 feet tall, but they are so broad and compact that they are, on average, almost as heavy as humans. Dwarves are considered adults at about age 50 and they can live to be over 400 years old.
Racial Hatreds: Orcs and Goblins

Avariel
 Playable Classes: ma (level 45), cl (level 40), wa (level 50), so (level 45)
Available Multiclasses: cl/wa, ma/wa, ma/cl, ma/cl/wa
Max Stats: 20 dex, 18 int, 18 wis, 17 con, 18 chr, 18 str
Avariel are a sub-species of elf, blessed with a pair of feathered wings. These wings are attached between their shoulder blades and range in color from white to black, with most being some shade of tufted grey. These wings are somewhat vulnerable to fire, and as a result Avariel tend to be somewhat paranoid about fire wielding mages and other natural sources of flame.
Racial Hatreds: None

Dark Elves
 Playable Classes: ma (level 50), cl (level 50), wa (level 30), th (level 40), dr (level 20),
mk (level 30), so (level 50), pa (level 40), ra (level 50), ps (level 40), ne (level 40)
Available Multiclasses: ma/wa, wa/th, ma/th, ma/cl, wa/ne, ma/wa/th
Max Stats: 20 dex, 18 int, 18 wis, 17 con, 17 chr, 18 str
Living in the cavernous Underdark, Dark elves spend most of their lives underground, protected from the sun's violent rays.  When they do surface, usually to make war on the surface races, they require protective magic for their bodies and their armor.  Dark elves split from the other elven races in time immemorial, but that doesn't keep them from hating one another.
Racial Hatreds: Moon elves, Wild elves, Gold elves
