Race Information

Havok has grown to become a very vast world.  Many different creatures and exotic races populate much of this world, all having there own traits and abilities .  As many as 132 races have been known to trek the lands.  To see a list of every race, type 'help allrace' while your on Havok.  But for a list and description of the most common playable races, see below.

Gold Elf - Sharp features such as pointed ears set this race apart from most. They are known to be flightly and not dependable. Elves are also known to be some of the best magicians around, as well as being on of the most dexterous races.
Prefered Class: Mage, Thief, Druid, Ranger
Racial Hatreds: Orcs
Max Stats: Str 18, Con 17, Dex 19, Int 19, Wis 17, Chr 18
Half Giant -  Race of half giant and half human, they look more human than anything and can often be confused as a large human. Half-Giants are known for their high strength and constitution.
Preferred Class: Barbarian, Warrior
Racial Hatreds: None
Max Stats: Int 17 , Dex 16 , Int 17 , Wis 17 , Con 18 , Chr 18 , Str 19
Human - Humans are really the most average of all the races.. They can advance in just about every class and have average stats.  What more would you like to know, I'm guessing your one too so I'm thinking there shouldn't' be much to know.
Preferred Class: All Classes
Racial Hatreds: None
Max Stats: All Stats 18
Half Ogre - Half Ogres are huge harry human looking race. They are not very smart nor very quick moving about, however they are strong and healthy.
Preferred Class: Warrior
Racial Hatreds: None
Max Stats: Int 17 , Dex 17 , Int 17 , Wis 18 , Con 19 , Chr 18 , Str 18
Dark-Elf- Same as elves, but higher dex, infravision, class limits are
different. Minus to hit in lighted rooms.
Preferred Class: Mage, Cleric, Rangers
Racial Hatreds: Moon Elves, Wild Elves, Gold Elves
Max Stats: Dex 20 , Int 18 , Wis 18 , Con 17 , Chr 17 , Str 18
Avariel Avariel are a sub-species of elf, blessed with a pair of feathered wings. These wings are attached between their shoulder blades and range in color from white to black, with most being some shade of tufted grey. These wings are somewhat vulnerable to fire, and as a result Avariel tend to be somewhat paranoid about fire wielding mages and other natural sources of flame.
Preferred Class: Warriors
Racial Hatreds: None
Max Stats: Dex 20 , Int 18 , Wis 18 , Con 17 , Chr 18 , Str 18

Dwarven Dwarves are known for their skill in warfare, their ability to withstand physical and magical punishment their knowledge of the earth's secrets, their hard work, and their capacity for drinking ale. They stand only 4 to 4 1/2 feet tall, but they are so broad and compact that they are, on average, almost as heavy as humans. Dwarves are considered adults at about age 50 and they can live to be over 400 years old.
Prefered Class: Warrior, Cleric, Wa/Cl
Racial Hatreds: Orcs and Goblins
Max Stats: Str 18, Con 19, Dex 17, Int 18, Wis 18, Chr 17
Halfling - Halflings are clever, capable opportunists. They stand about three feet tall and usually weigh between 30 and 35 pounds. Often they are strangers and wanderers, and others react to them with suspicion or curiosity.
Preferred Class: Thief
Racial Hatreds: None
Max Stats: Str 17, Con 18, Dex 19, Int 18, Wis 18, Chr 18
